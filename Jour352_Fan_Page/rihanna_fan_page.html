Who is Rihanna?

Most people know Rihanna as a singer. However, she holds many other titles. She is also an actress, businesswoman, and songwriter. Rihanna was born as Robyn Rihanna Fenty in Barbados. She became famous worldwide for her versatile music. Rihanna has R&B, pop, reggae, dancehall, and hip-hop songs. She has also stared in movies like Guava Island, Ocean's 8, and Battleship. She also launched beauty products, skin products, and a clothing line. All of these businesses are successful today because they were designed to be inclusive and diverse.
