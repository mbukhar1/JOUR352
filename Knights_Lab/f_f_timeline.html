Fast & Furious Franchise Timeline

The Fast and Furious movies have become really popular. Since they started in 2001, people all over the world have loved the exciting action, cool car chases, and the different characters in the movies. What started as a movie about street racing has become a huge success, making billions of dollars at the movie theaters. The movies keep getting better because they try new things, like heists and spy stuff, while still keeping the important ideas of family, loyalty, and lots of excitement. Each new movie has even crazier stunts and awesome moments that make people really excited. The movies have a lot of fans who have loved them for a long time and are always excited for the next one. The Fast and Furious movies are successful because they have exciting action, interesting characters, and they always make sure to give the audience a thrilling experience that makes them want more.
